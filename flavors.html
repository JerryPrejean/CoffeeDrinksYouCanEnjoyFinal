    Now, the flavors typically depend on the coffee shop
    Most have a menu, but a lot of them should have the same
    types.

    Now that you have the three, the main part
    will be to order. Depending on the shop
    You can order it in a step process.

    "Can I have a (Coffee) that is (Style) with
    (Flavor) please?"

    Savory -
    Caramel.
    Vanilla
    Mocha
    Hazelnut
    French Vanilla.
    Almond

    Fruit/Plant -
    Raspberry
    Lavender
    Coconut
    Strawberry
    Banana

    Cold Brew and Iced Coffee are both typically
    Plain and you have to add the flavor to it.
    If not you will get an unsweetened default
    most of the time.

    A latte is typically espresso with milk
    added. most of the time it comes with dairy
    used milk, but you can always ask for an alternative
